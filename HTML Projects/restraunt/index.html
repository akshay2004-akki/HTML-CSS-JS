Burger

1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil grate.
2. Whisk together egg, salt, and pepper in a medium bowl.
3. Add ground beef and bread crumbs and mix with your hands or a fork until well blended.
4. Form into four 3/4-inch-thick patties.
5. Place patties on the preheated grill.
6. Serve hot and enjoy!

4. Now, make the cappuccino!

Cold Coffee

1. Mix ground coffee with water in a large container.
2. Line a fine mesh strainer with cheesecloth and set over a pitcher or other container.
3. Place coffee liquid in the fridge and allow to cool.
4. To make iced coffee, pack a glass full of ice cubes.

4. Fry the Potatoes.
5. Drain and Cool the Half-Done Fries.
6. Fry the Potatoes Again.
7. Drain the Fries.

Garlic Potatoes

1. Place potatoes in a large saucepan and cover with 2 inches of cold water. Generously salt the water. Place the pan over high heat, bring to a boil, and cook at an active simmer until they're fork-tender. Drain.
2. Toss the warm potatoes with olive oil and salt. Garnish as you like and serve.

Kulcha

1. Kulcha is made from maida flour, water, a pinch of salt and a leavening agent (yeast or old kulcha dough), mixed together by hand to make a very tight dough.
2. This dough is covered with a wet cloth and left to stand for an hour or so in a warm place.
3. The result is a slight leavening of the dough but not much.

Maggie

1. Add 2 cups of water to a kettle.
2. Take a large bowl.
3. Cut the masala packets and empty them into the bowl.
4. Now pour the boiling water into the bowl.
5. Use a fork to break the maggi into parts and mix it well in the masala water.

Manchurian

1. firstly, in a large bowl take 1 carrot, 5 tbsp cabbage, 5 tbsp spring onion, ½ onion and ½ tsp ginger garlic paste.
2. also add 1 tsp chilli sauce, ½ tsp salt.
3. mix well making sure everything is combined well.
4. further, add 2 tbsp maida, 2 tbsp cornflour and mix well.

Mojito

1. Lightly muddle the mint with the simple syrup in a shaker.
2. Add the rum, lime juice and ice, and give it a brief shake, then strain into a highball glass over fresh ice.
3. Top with the club soda.
4. Garnish with a mint sprig and lime wheel.

Momos

1. Finely cut all vegetables as mentioned in the ingredients section.
2. Add 3/4 cup maida (all purpose flour), 1 teaspoon oil and salt in a large bowl.
3. Mix all ingredients well and prepare a soft pliable dough (like paratha dough) by adding water as needed.
4. Heat 1 tablespoon oil in a pan over medium flame.

Oreo shake

1. Soften the ice cream and chill the glass. Take the ice cream out of the freezer for 10 to 15 minutes before use.
2. Measure the ingredients. 1 cup ice cream to 1/4 cup milk is a great ratio.
3. Blend the shake. In a blender, combine the milk and ice cream and blend until smooth.
4. Serve.

Tomato Soup

1. Melt butter over medium heat in a Dutch oven or large saucepan.
2. Add onion wedges, water, can of tomatoes with their juices, and 1/2 teaspoon of salt. Bring to a simmer.
3. Blend the soup, and then season to taste. The soup doesn't need to be ultra-smooth, some texture is a nice touch.

Wrap

4. With each fold, make sure you secure the filling by pressing it down gently.

Egg Fried Rice

1. Heat the oil until hot and pour in the beaten eggs.
2. Wait until the bottom sets and scramble lightly.
3. Add the rice when the eggs are mostly set but partially runny.
4. Cook and stir to chop the eggs into smaller bits.
5. Add the green onion and salt.
6. Thoroughly toast the rice.

About Us

Welcome to Taste of India, where culinary artistry meets warm hospitality. Nestled in the heart of Hisar, our restaurant is a haven for food enthusiasts seeking a memorable dining experience.


Our Story

Founded in [2018], TOI was born out of a passion for creating exceptional cuisine and a desire to share it with our community. Our journey began with a simple vision: to craft dishes that tantalize the taste buds, using only the finest, locally-sourced ingredients.


Our Culinary Philosophy

At TOI, we believe that food should be a celebration of flavors, textures, and creativity. Our talented team of chefs draws inspiration from global cuisines, infusing classic recipes with innovative twists to create a menu that appeals to every palate. We take pride in our commitment to sustainability and environmental responsibility. Our dedication to sourcing ingredients locally ensures that our dishes are not only delicious but also support local farmers and producers. We also strive to minimize waste and energy consumption in our restaurant operations.
